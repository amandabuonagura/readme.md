Street artist Banksy



His artwork can be seen all around the world, though we may never be sure of his identity. His artwork can be seen all around the world. His graffiti style work is controversial, and quite impactful to say the least. He inspires me because he makes art for the purpose of spreadying powerful messages about society, government, and life in general.Banksy has impacted not only the street/urban art movement, but to contemporary art in general. Similar to Warhol, he uses iconic images that are relatable, only he adds a twist that tells a story and makes you think deeply.
